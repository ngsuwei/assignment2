GYMMBOX

Established in October 2010,
GYMMBOXX started as a single gym situated at the top of
a multi-storey car park in Singapore (Bedok Reservoir).

Over the years, GYMMBOXX has made its mark
as Singapore’s affordable premium gym brand,
serving the residential neighbourhoods and heartlands.
With more than 7 gyms (and counting) island-wide,
GYMMBOXX will continue to expand and bring quality gym facilities
and service to the rest of Singapore and beyond.


Our Principles of GYMMBOXX


VARIABLE MEMBERSHIP PLANS

We provide long and short term membership plans
to give our users of all ages the freedom to best suits their fitness needs.
TRANSPARENT FIXED RATES

Providing transparency to our pricing
is a testament to our confidence in giving the most affordable price plans.
SAFETY FIRST

We are the only constantly manned gym
in Singapore that provides a 24-hour access. This is to ensure the safety of our members
even during the wee hours of the night.
